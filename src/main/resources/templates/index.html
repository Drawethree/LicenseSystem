License System

Create and Manage licenses for your premium software.

What is License System about?

License system is a web application where you can create and manage licenses for customers using your software. Your customers need to register an account on our site to redeem their licenses.

 Register an account

Using as Creator

After registering your account, start by creating a software in our user panel. In user panel you can create licenses for your software and give the license keys to your customers to redeem.

Using as Customer

Have you purchased a license from one of our creators? Lets redeem your license! You can also view all of your licenses in our customer panel.
